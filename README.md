# Rami The Barber

Website for **Rami The Barber** — a barber shop in Ottawa (Barrhaven). Precision fades, sharp lineups, beard trims. Book your appointment in seconds.

**ramithebarber.com**

---

**Home** — Hero, about Rami (photo, bio, hobby, stats), services (Haircut & Beard, Haircut), before/after transformations, gallery, Game Tape videos, reviews, location & contact, footer with booking CTA.

**Book** — Full-page Square booking. You can also open booking from the nav or from the “Book Now” buttons across the site.

**Privacy** — Privacy policy page.

Media (hero image, gallery, transformations, videos, about photo, OG image) is served from Supabase Storage; booking is powered by Square.
